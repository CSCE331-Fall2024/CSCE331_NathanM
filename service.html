Easter Egg Hunt

This was one of my favorite things I set up with my martial arts school. We reserved a spot at a park, and set up an easter egg hunt for the community to come join. There were multiple age groups so the older kids did not crowd the younger ones. All of the kids had fun and made great memories with their family! Many of the families also signed their kids up for my martial art's After School Program Check it out!

Halloween Trick or Treat in ASEC
